    • About me

    Hello, my name is Welson. I am a skilled and passionate barber from Brazil. With my barbering journey starting in 2019 and completing my formal training in 2020, I have developed a strong foundation in the art of hair cutting. I specialize in creating flawless fades, showcasing my precision and attention to detail. Alongside fades, I also excel in delivering classic cuts, catering to a variety of styles and preferences. My commitment to providing exceptional service and ensuring client satisfaction has helped me build a loyal customer base. I am dedicated, creative, and constantly seeking opportunities to enhance my skills in this ever-evolving industry. I am excited to bring my expertise and enthusiasm to new professional challenges in the field of barbering.
